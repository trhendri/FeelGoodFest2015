About

Feel Good Fest will bring various local artists and musicians together with community members for an event that is not a conventional concert or performance, but rather, a transformative visual and auditory experience designed to attract a wide audience. This event will focus heavily on art installations and provide selected local artists a temporary space in which to present their work. Feel Good Fest is a not-for-profit event surviving solely on donations and fundraising events. If you would like to make a contribution towards making Feel Good Fest fantastic, please contact Robert Randow at citizensofthecountryside@gmail.com.

For more information about getting involved, click the contact link at the top of this page.

Robert Randow

Robert Randow is a self proscribed hypnotist of musical meandering, a grower of growling gardens, and is also the creator of what is Feel Good Fest.

Melissa Tamporello

Melissa Tamporello delves in animation, printmaking, and custom picture framing. She organizes the visual arts and other social media aspects for the fest.
